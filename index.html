GlucoTrackr

Get Started

Open-source MIT Licensed.

Glucose Trend Graph 🛡️

Get a beautiful, interactive line graph that shows your glucose levels across the past 24 hours. Spot trends, track patterns, and make data-driven decisions about your health.

Smart Alerts 🚀

Instantly know when your glucose is outside your safe range. Receive personalized, non-intrusive alerts that help you take action before it becomes serious.

Personalized Insights 📈

GlucoTrackr learns your patterns and provides weekly summaries and suggestions to help improve glucose stability based on your lifestyle and routines.

Nighttime Low Detection 🦉

Sleep with peace of mind. GlucoTrackr automatically monitors nighttime data to detect hypoglycemia and helps prevent dangerous overnight lows.

PDF Export for Doctors ✨

With one click, generate a professional PDF report of your glucose trends. Easily share it with your doctor for your next appointment or ongoing care.

Caregiver Dashboard Access🧰

Invite trusted caregivers or family members to view your dashboard. Give them peace of mind with read-only access to real-time health data.
